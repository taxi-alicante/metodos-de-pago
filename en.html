Available Options
💶 CARD, MOBILE PAYMENT OR CASH

We accept the following conventional payment methods directly in the vehicle:

  • Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.
  • Mobile Payment: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Bizum Pay.
  • Revolut: Revtag.
  • Cash: Local currency (Euros).
₿ PAY WITH BITCOIN (BTC)

We accept Bitcoin payments adapted to the type of trip for maximum security and speed:

1. Urban Trips (Taximeter)

Payment is made upon arrival using Lightning Network (processed through Coinos). We will generate a dynamic QR code on the smartphone screen; the payment settles in 1 second with almost zero network fees.

2. Intercity or Airport Trips (Fixed Price)

If you request the Fixed Price option through the PIDETAXI App, we allow direct On-Chain (Main Network) payment to the wallet.

We set the amount in Satoshis and you make the transfer when getting in the car; the trip (minimum 10-15 min) provides the necessary time to process the confirmation on the Blockchain during the journey.

Recommended Wallets:
